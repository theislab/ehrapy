MIMIC-II IAC Introduction#

This tutorial explores the MIMIC-II IAC dataset. It was created for the purpose of a case study in the book: Secondary Analysis of Electronic Health Records, published by Springer in 2016. In particular, the dataset was used throughout Chapter 16 (Data Analysis) by Raffa J. et al.Â to investigate the effectiveness of indwelling arterial catheters in hemodynamically stable patients with respiratory failure for mortality outcomes. The dataset is derived from MIMIC-II, the publicly-accessible critical care database. It contains summary clinical data and outcomes for 1,776 patients.

More details on the dataset can be found here: https://physionet.org/content/mimic2-iaccd/1.0/.
